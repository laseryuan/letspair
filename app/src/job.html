Job Description - Coding Tutor/Mentor

Company Description

Let's Pair is an innovative start-up dedicated to enhancing coding skills through the practice of pair programming. We provide a unique learning environment combining co-working spaces with a coding bootcamp experience. Our mission is to support learners and developers by pairing them together to work on real-life projects, fostering a collaborative and enjoyable learning process. Our first center is located in the hub of Vancouver downtown just steps away from Granville Station and Vancouver City Center Station.

We are building an initial team to kick off the business. You will be part of a dynamic team driven by creativity and innovation. If you are excited with the opportunity to fulfill your dream of making critical changes and setting new standards for the industry, we look forward to hearing from you.

Role Description

This is a full-time on-site role for a Coding Tutor/Mentor at Let's Pair Education in Vancouver, BC. The Coding Tutor/Mentor will be responsible for providing guidance, support, and mentorship to learners and junior developers seeking to improve their coding skills. The role involves creating learning projects, conducting pair coding sessions, and tracking learning progress. The role works collaboratively with learners on real-life projects, implementing and promoting pair programming methodology; developing and maintaining company website, matching engine and commercial software applications.

The term is negotiable to be full-time, part-time, or internship.

Qualifications

  • Proficiency in web development languages i.e. Html, JavaScript or CSS
  • Experience in teaching, tutoring, or mentoring students in coding is a strong asset
  • Strong communication skills and ability to explain complex concepts clearly
  • Patient, empathetic, and supportive attitude towards learners
  • Ability to adapt pair coding methods to suit the learning styles of individual learners
  • Ability to work well in a team and collaborate effectively
  • Willing to work in a start-up environment with growth mindset
  • Passion for education, coding, and helping others succeed
  • Previous experience in software development is a plus
  • Relevant certifications in coding or education are beneficial

Remuneration

Salary: $30-$40 per hour, 37.5 hours per week, $58,500-$78,000 per year

+ Extended health benefit

+ Transit allowance

+ Free fitness membership
